## Acceptance Questions For A Future Review

- Are all six input pins in a known safe state at reset?
- Does the confirmed DT/MODE state match the firmware signal strategy?
- Is deadtime generated by exactly one reviewed mechanism?
- Are same-leg high-side and low-side Gate commands non-overlapping?
- Does nFAULT remain high except for documented startup behavior?
- Does REG12 remain above its valid operating threshold without abnormal droop?
- Are all measurements made within probe common-mode, differential and category ratings?

## Stop/Return Conditions For A Future Task

These conditions terminate a future dynamic review; they do not instruct the user to perform it now:

- nFAULT goes low unexpectedly.
- Supply changes from CV to CC or current rises unexpectedly.
- REG12 falls or becomes unstable.
- Any smell, sound, visible damage or fast heating appears.
- Ground clip placement is uncertain.
- Same-leg overlap is suspected.
- Firmware commit, pin map or DT/MODE evidence does not match the reviewed plan.
